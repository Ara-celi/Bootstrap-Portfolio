About Me

My name is Araceli, pronounced (Are-ah-cell-lee). It means heavens alter in Greek; although, I'm not Greek. I learned the meaning of my name thanks to the power of the internet, yeay technology. Before I discovered the search engine (yes I have aged myself) people would ask me if I knew what my name meant and I would say 'no'. One man told me that in Latin, Ara meant bird, so for years I thought my name meant bird (something). I would like to say he was on the right path, I guess...

I love traveling, being outdoors and staying active. I have visited almost all 50 states and 10 countries abroad, some more than once. A 5k is a normal run for me and a short bike ride is 20 miles. I'm not a very strong swimmer but with all the shark and gator attacks and amoeba in the lakes I'm okay with that. Paddleboards, kayaks and boats make for a perfect day on the water. Hiking is on the top of my favorite things to do but living in Florida puts a damper on that list. There's pros and cons to everything.
